My teaching philosophy is student-focused, and my goal is to make my classes a mathematically fulfilling experience that prepares each person to attain their goals.

The math department at the University of Kentucky has recognized my teaching with the Wimberly C. Royster Outstanding Teaching Assistant Award and by giving me opportunities to act as Lead TA for MA123 and work with the MathExcel program.

To see what my past students have to say about my classes, look here.
