My research interests lie in the field of algebraic geometry. In particular, most of my work so far has been using tools from tropical geometry to study the scrollar invariants of curves. I also like to think about algebra and combinatorics, especially coding theory, tableaux, and graph theory, and am fascinated by things counted by the Catalan numbers.
